# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I create an event?
Click "Create Event" and fill in the form with event details.

## Can I edit my event?
Yes, click on your event and select "Edit".

## How do I upload photos?
Go to event details and click "Upload Photo".

## Can I delete my comments?
Yes, only you can delete your own comments.

## How do I get notifications?
Enable notifications in your preferences.

## What categories are available?
BMW, JDM, Classics, Drift, Tuning, Racing, Meetup, Track Day.

## Is there a mobile app?
Not yet, but it's planned for v1.2.

## How do I report an issue?
Contact support or open an issue on GitHub.
